üìù Interpretation Guide

Reading Character Evolution Charts:

  • Steep upward slopes: Rapid character growth (look for catalyst episodes)
  • Plateaus: Stable character phase (consolidation period)
  • Downward trends: Regression or strategic retreat (rare but significant)

Example: Alessandra's confidence jumps from 3‚Üí6 between Ep 20-33 because of:

  • Episode 21: Horse excrement revenge
  • Episode 22: Physical confrontation with Katrina
  • Episode 23: Demands stolen inheritance
  • Episode 33: Survives abduction attempt

Reading Relationship Charts:

  • Parallel lines: Balanced relationship growth
  • Diverging lines: Tension or imbalance
  • Converging lines: Resolution or harmony

Example: Edgar & Alessandra's intimacy and trust lines converge at Ep 89 (consummation) - physical and emotional intimacy finally aligned.

Reading Power Dynamics:

  • Score of 2-4: Antagonist has power over Alessandra
  • Score of 5-6: Power struggle, uncertain outcome
  • Score of 7-8: Alessandra gaining advantage
  • Score of 9-10: Alessandra has complete power (victory)

Note: Score of 10 can mean different things:

  • Kate (Ep 21): Alessandra humiliated her, power inverted
  • Desmond (Ep 34): Alessandra ordered his execution
  • Priscilla (Ep 93): Alessandra defeated her financially
